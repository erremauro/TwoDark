TwoDark Changelog
==================

## [1.1.1] 2016-12-08

### Changed

* Scrollbars background color has been darkened to match the general ui palette. Unlike Atom, Sublime Text use a unique texture for every scrollbars background. This doesn't affect users using scrollbar overlay.

## [1.1.0] 2016-12-08

### Add

* Scrollbars (Whoa, magical!)
* Support for 'Skins' plugin.
